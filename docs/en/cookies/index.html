Cookie
Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as login status, preferences, and browsing behavior.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Booster Bundle uses only essential functional cookies:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Required?
Session Cookies Maintain login state and session data Session (deleted when browser closes) Yes
Authentication Cookies Verify your identity and account access 30 days (or while logged in) Yes
Preference Cookies Remember your language and display settings 1 year No

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Google Analytics: traffic analysis (optional — you can opt-out)
  • Shopify: payment processing and cart management
  • GoHighLevel: CRM and marketing automation

These services comply with GDPR and ePrivacy regulations.

4. Do We Track You?

No. We do not track your behavior across websites. Our cookies are used only within Booster Bundle for:

  • Keeping you logged in securely
  • Processing your purchases
  • Remembering your preferences

5. How to Manage Cookies

You can control cookies in your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data

Warning: Disabling essential cookies may prevent you from logging in or accessing your account.

6. Consent and Opt-Out

We use only essential cookies without requiring explicit consent (as permitted by GDPR). If we use analytics cookies in the future, we will request your consent first.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy. Changes become effective upon posting. Your continued use of Booster Bundle constitutes acceptance of updated policies.
