Your Right to Erasure

Under the GDPR (Article 17) and Spanish law, you have the right to request complete deletion of your personal data from our systems at any time. We call this the "Right to Erasure."

What Does This Mean?

We delete:

  • Your name, email, and phone
  • Purchase history and content access
  • Navigation data and IP linked to your account
  • Any personally identifiable information

Legal Exceptions: we retain financial transaction records for 6 years (required by tax law).

How to Request Deletion

You have 3 ways to request data deletion:

1. Direct Email

Send an email to legal@boosterbundle.digital with the subject:

Subject: "Data Deletion Request - GDPR Art. 17"

In your email include:

  • Your full name
  • The email associated with your account
  • Confirmation that you want to delete all your data

2. Online Form

Complete the following form and we will process it automatically:

Data Deletion Form

⚠️ Important: This action is irreversible. Once deleted, we cannot recover your data. You will have access to your account for 30 more days to download any content.

3. Through Your Account

In your Booster Bundle user dashboard:

  1. Go to Settings → Privacy and Data
  2. Click "Request Account Deletion"
  3. Confirm your request

Response Timeline

30 Calendar Days

We will respond to your request within maximum 30 days (GDPR Art. 12). If your request is complex, we may extend to 90 days and will notify you by email.

No Cost: The process is completely free.

What Happens Next

  • We delete all your personal data from our systems
  • Instructions to our vendors (Shopify, GoHighLevel, etc.) to delete their copies
  • Email confirmation when deletion is complete
  • Your access to the platform is automatically deactivated

Exceptions (Data We Retain)

By law, we retain:

  • Tax Records: 6 years (required by tax law)
  • Security Records: if fraud is suspected (maximum 1 year)
  • Anonymized Data: for business statistics (not identifiable)

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my mind?

Yes, within the first 30 days of your request. Contact us to cancel it. After deletion, data cannot be recovered.

What about my purchases?

Your purchase history is anonymized (only date and amount retained, not linked to you). You cannot access previously downloaded content.

What about my social media data?

We delete your data from our systems, but not from platforms like Facebook or Google. You must request deletion from those services directly.

How do you verify my identity?

We will send a verification link to your registered email. Only by accessing that link do you confirm ownership of the account.
