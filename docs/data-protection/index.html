5. Data Subject Rights

Under GDPR and Spanish law, you have irrevocable rights:

Right of Access (Art. 15 GDPR)

Request a copy of your personal data. We will respond within 30 days at no cost.

Right of Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)

Correct inaccurate or incomplete data immediately.

Right to Erasure / Right to Be Forgotten (Art. 17 GDPR)

Request deletion of your data. We will comply except for legal obligations (tax records, 6 years).

Right to Restrict Processing (Art. 18 GDPR)

Limit the use of your data while you dispute its accuracy.

Right to Data Portability (Art. 20 GDPR)

Receive your data in structured format (CSV, JSON) to transfer to another controller.

Right to Object (Art. 21 GDPR)

Refuse processing based on legitimate interest or for marketing.
